How to Play

Master the art of UWA navigation in 3 easy steps.

1. Study the Image

You'll be shown a high-resolution photo of a location somewhere on the UWA campus. Look for clues like building architecture, specific trees, or recognizable statues.

2. Pin the Map

Once you think you know where the photo was taken, click on the interactive campus map. You can zoom in to find specific wings or garden paths.

3. Check Your Accuracy

The closer your pin is to the actual location, the more points you earn! You'll see the exact distance between your guess and the true spot.
